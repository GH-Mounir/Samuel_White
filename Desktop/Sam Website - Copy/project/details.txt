BIOGRAPHY

سموءل سخية، مخرج ومنتج مستقل من أصل سوري، 
وُلِد في عام 1987 في اللاذقية، سوريا. درس الاقتصاد. بدأ مسيرته 
الفنية كممثل ومخرج في المسرح الجامعي ومشاريع 
مستقلة مختلفة. بدأت شغفه بالسينما منذ صغره،
 وتأثر كثيرًا بفيلم "العراب" لفرانسيس فورد كوبولا، الذي 
أصبح مرجعًا فنيًا وإنتاجيًا له. نظرًا لعدم وجود مدارس سينمائية في سوريا،
بدأ في صنع أفلامه الخاصة بشكل مستقل وطوّر مهاراته بشكل ذاتي.
أخرج فيلمين قصيرين في سوريا قبل أن ينتقل إلى تونس
 في عام 2016 خلال الحرب السورية، مستمرًا في صنع أفلامه 
القصيرة المستقلة. بين عامي 2017 و2018، أخرج ثلاثة أفلام قصيرة.
عمل على إنتاج وإخراج مختبر سينمائي-مسرحي بعنوان 
"العنبر رقم 6"، الذي استند إلى قصة أنطون تشيخوف الشهيرة.
 لقي هذا المشروع استحسانًا واسعًا من المجتمع الفني والثقافي
، حيث استعرض الأبعاد الإنسانية والنفسية للشخصيات 
في إطار مستشفى للأمراض العقلية.

BIOGRAPHY
Samuel Skhieh, an independent filmmaker and producer of Syrian origin, was born in 1987 in Latakia, Syria. He studied economics. He began his career as an actor and director in university theater and various independent projects. His passion for cinema began early, and he was greatly influenced by the film "The Godfather" by Francis Ford Coppola, which became an artistic and productive reference for him. Since there were no film schools in Syria, he started making his own films independently and developed his skills in a self-taught manner. He directed two short films in Syria before moving to Tunisia in 2016 during the Syrian war, continuing to make his independent short films. Between 2017 and 2018, he directed three short films. He worked on the production and direction of a cinematic-theatrical laboratory titled “Ward - N°6", which received widespread acclaim from the artistic and cultural community. He then ventured into directing music videos and commercials with famous artists, garnering millions of views. He also created a remarkable experience with the violinist and composer Mohamed Al-Gharbi in a two-part musical short film titled "Son of the ground", the first of its kind in the Arab world celebrating instrumental music. He made his debut in series by directing and writing the first episode of the series "Path of Death". He worked on the production, direction, and writing of his first feature-length narrative film titled "The Return", 35% of which was filmed in 2021 due to the coronavirus pandemic. Upon returning to Syria, he produced and directed a short documentary titled "Souls on the Other STRAND", which was particularly appreciated by judging committees. He is currently working on his first feature-Long documentary, which is in post-production.
